# Payslip Celebration Platform MVP

Welcome to the Payslip Celebration Platform!

## Overview
The Payslip Celebration Platform is designed to help individuals celebrate milestone achievements in their careers, marked by the receipt of paychecks or payslips from their employers. This platform encourages a community of sharing, support, and appreciation for professional growth.

## Features
- **User Profiles**: Users can create profiles to share their career journeys and milestones.
- **Celebration Posts**: Users can post celebrations with their payslip details and personal messages.
- **Community Interaction**: Features to comment on and appreciate other users' achievements.
- **Notifications**: Alerts for upcoming celebrations and community events.

## Getting Started
1. **Sign Up**: Create an account on the platform.
2. **Create a Celebration Post**: Share your latest payslip moment with the community!
3. **Engage**: Interact with other users by commenting and sharing your thoughts.

## Technologies Used
- **Frontend**: ReactJS
- **Backend**: Node.js with Express
- **Database**: MongoDB
